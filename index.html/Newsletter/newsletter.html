Recipe of the Month

Lemon Blueberry Loaf

Our bright and cheerful spring specialty combines tart lemon with sweet blueberries in a moist, tender loaf.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar

Tip: Toss blueberries in flour before adding to batter to prevent sinking!
