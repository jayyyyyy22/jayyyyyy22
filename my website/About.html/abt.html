Our Story

It all started with a bag of flour and a whole lot of love. In the early months of 2020, while the world paused, our founder Jayanne Kennedy found comfort in the rhythm of baking. What began as a personal escape soon became a neighborhood obsession ‚Äî friends and family couldn't get enough of her warm sourdough and golden croissants. By the end of that year, The Flour House opened its doors as a cozy corner bakery with a big heart.

Since then, we've grown into a beloved bakery known for artisanal craftsmanship, friendly service, and mouthwatering treats that bring people together. Our goal has always been simple: make people feel at home with every bite.

Our Baking Philosophy

We believe baking is both an art and a science ‚Äî and that every good bake starts with intention. That's why we focus on small batches, long fermentations, and natural ingredients. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromise. We source our flour from trusted local mills, use farm-fresh butter and eggs, and take our time with every recipe.

From our 24-hour sourdough loaves to our flaky pastries, everything is made in-house, by hand, with patience and pride. Because food tastes better when it's made with love.
